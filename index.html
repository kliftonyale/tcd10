the caribbean digital X

December 7–8, 2023

Yale University | New Haven



The Caribbean Digital conference (TCD) is an anual international event, held in locations in the United States and the Caribbean since 2014. This year, we celebrate our 10th anniversary at Yale University in New Haven, Connecticut. On December 7 and 8, we will come together for a rich collection of panels, showcases, and keynote dialogues. Our panelists will delve into theory, pedagogy, praxis and archives. Two rounds of showcases will highlight new work from our communities of praxis around the Caribbean and its diasporas. The two-day event is open to all interested in the intersection between technology and the history and culture of the Caribbean. We hope you can join us!
